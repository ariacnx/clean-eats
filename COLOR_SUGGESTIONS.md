# Icon Color Suggestions for Japanese Minimalist Aesthetic

Here are some color options that would work well with your amber/yellow card backgrounds while maintaining a Japanese minimalist feel:

## Option 1: Deep Indigo (#4A5568 or #2D3748)
- Traditional Japanese color (indigo dye)
- Creates beautiful contrast with warm yellow
- Very elegant and sophisticated
- Hex: `#4A5568` (slate-600) or `#2D3748` (slate-700)

## Option 2: Charcoal Gray (#374151 or #1F2937)
- Subtle and refined
- Classic Japanese aesthetic
- Works beautifully with warm tones
- Hex: `#374151` (gray-700) or `#1F2937` (gray-800)

## Option 3: Soft Sage Green (#6B7280 or #4B5563)
- Natural and calming
- Complements warm yellow beautifully
- Earthy and grounded
- Hex: `#6B7280` (gray-500) or `#4B5563` (gray-600)

## Option 4: Warm Terracotta (#C2410C or #B45309)
- Earthy complement to yellow
- Warm and inviting
- Natural color harmony
- Hex: `#C2410C` (orange-700) or `#B45309` (amber-700)

## Option 5: Muted Teal (#0F766E or #0D9488)
- Calming and sophisticated
- Beautiful contrast with yellow
- Modern yet timeless
- Hex: `#0F766E` (teal-700) or `#0D9488` (teal-600)

## Option 6: Deep Burgundy (#7F1D1D or #991B1B)
- Rich and elegant
- Traditional Japanese color
- Creates sophisticated contrast
- Hex: `#7F1D1D` (red-900) or `#991B1B` (red-800)
